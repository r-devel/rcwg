### Translation consistency

* Could we use LLMs to identify inconsistent translation of terms? 
* Saranjeet Kaur got some R Consortium funding to work on a package to help keep glossaries in sync and to share contributions with other projects (rOpenSci, Bioconductor, The Carpentries)
* Also have translation community documentation: https://contributor.r-project.org/translations. If have good documented examples, could point an LLM to it to learn how to translate R messages well.

    * LLM could then be used to translate messages in CRAN/GitHub packages - only practical way to do at scale.

    * Translation team can then focus on pre-training LLM on high quality translations in Weblate

* Maybe in future, LLMs could translate messages at install/run time
