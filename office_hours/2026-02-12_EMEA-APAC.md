### Guides on using S4 methods

* Guidance is not to use braces when defining S4 method as that is not so efficient

    * Not intuitive that formatting has an effect, which is not usually case in R

* Hugo working on blog post going through different cases

    * May not have a performance impact in R these days, possibly out-of-date advice

* Ella did raise at this an office hour after earlier discussion with Hugo, where Michael Lawrence gave an initial response: https://github.com/r-devel/rcwg/blob/main/office_hours/2025-12-11-AMER.md
